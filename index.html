KFC Restaurant Closing at Irving TX

Executive Summary

- Strategic Location Advantage: Pollo Campero's close proximity to the closing KFC, along with its high food quality and service ratings, positions it well to attract displaced KFC customers. Its superior ratings in food quality (4.76) and service (4.63) compared to nearby competitors like El Ranchero and Church's Chicken can be leveraged to draw in new patrons.

- Drive-Thru and Delivery Focus: Given the high reliance on drive-thru sales (49.31%) at the Irving location, Pollo Campero should capitalize on this by ensuring efficient and appealing drive-thru operations to attract former KFC customers who prefer this convenience. Additionally, the higher-than-average delivery sales (1.58%) present an opportunity to further invest in and promote delivery services to capture customers seeking home delivery options.

- Addressing Service and Menu Issues: To maximize the opportunity, Pollo Campero should address specific service complaints and improve the taste of its grilled chicken. By enhancing these areas, Pollo Campero can strengthen its overall customer satisfaction and better compete with other nearby options, ensuring it becomes the preferred choice for former KFC customers.
